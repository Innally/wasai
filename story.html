Our Story

One mountain's inheritance, shared with the world.

WASAI brings together tea craft built over generations in East Half Mountain, Mengku, Yunnan. We work with 700+ farming households, honoring local knowledge while upholding high standards in every stage from tea garden to storage.

Our practices ban pesticide and chemical fertilizers in contracted gardens, rely on manual garden care, and use traditional sun-dried processing techniques. The result is tea that is clean, expressive, and connected to land and people.
